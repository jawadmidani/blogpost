1. Risk Assessment Procedures

A Risk Assessment identifies, evaluates, and prioritises risks to critical assets. Below are the key steps:

Step 1: Identify and Categorise Assets

  • Physical Assets: Servers, routers, laptops
  • Digital Assets: Databases, software, cloud infrastructure
  • Human Assets: Employees and stakeholders

2. ISO/IEC 27001: Risk Management Framework

ISO/IEC 27001 is an internationally recognised standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS). Its key features include:

  • Risk-Based Approach: Identifies and treats risks systematically.
  • Security Controls: Covers organisational, physical, and technical security.
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular audits and reviews.

3. Data Protection Processes and Regulations

Organisations like Vodafone must comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. Key principles include:

  • Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency
  • Purpose Limitation
  • Data Minimisation
  • Accuracy and Storage Limitation
  • Integrity and Confidentiality

Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP)

A Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP) ensures systems are restored after disruptions. Components include:

  • Risk Analysis: Identify and prioritise risks.
  • Recovery Objectives: Define RTO and RPO.
  • Backup Procedures: Steps to restore data.
  • Roles and Responsibilities: Clear team assignments.
  • Testing and Training: Regularly validate the DRP.
